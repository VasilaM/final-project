About Us! Study Better is a course based in Khorog, Tajikistan, available for high school and college students seeking to improve and make their studying. It offers a month-long program in which it teaches how to spend less time studying and more time doing things you enjoy as well techniques for how to get the most of your studying sessions. The program was founded by two Khorog high school students who want to share their knowledge on gaining knowledge after extensive research and self-trial. Study better wants to help you become an excellent student and do all the thing you love at the same time!
