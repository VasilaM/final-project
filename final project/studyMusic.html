studyMusic


Piano Music

A great choice for calming, slow and steady music is piano music. Many love to listen to the soothing sound of a piano while studying.

Lo-Fi

A personal favorite of the founders of study better, lo-fi is really nice background music, perfect for studying and chilling to.

Sounds of Nature

Research has proven that sounds of nature make people feel at ease, making them great background music for prepping for a test.

String Instruments

The classical sound of string instruments, just like that of the piano, is also a slow, non-distracting option for background music.
